About Me

Responsible AI | NLP & AI Fairness | AI for Social Good

Welcome! I am a PhD candidate in Natural Language Processing at RMIT University, specialising in fairness and bias mitigation in NLP systems. My research focuses on developing responsible AI solutions that address equity concerns in automated systems.

Research

My research explores fairness and bias mitigation in Natural Language Processing. I develop evaluation methodologies and mitigation techniques to ensure AI systems produce equitable outcomes across diverse populations and viewpoints.
